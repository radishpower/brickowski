Furniture Manufacturer's collections are created for a discerning customer that values quality, craftsmanship, authenticity, and, above all, good design. We produce singular pieces, and aim to make each one the purest expression of its original design

HISTORY

The Furniture Manufacturer's story began in November of 2006, when its founder, designer John Doe, found himself puzzled that there were so few International companies represented among the leaders in international design. Six sleepless months later Furniture Manufacturer's debut collection launched at the International Contemporary Furniture Fair (ICFF) in New York.

The following year Furniture Manufacturer showed collections in New York and at the Milan Furniture Fair, and a year later found itself the recipient of the coveted ICFF Editors Award for Furniture. Still only four years young, Furniture Manufacturer has great expectations for the future, and we look forward to bringing you great design for years to come.

Jimmy Delover is the Founder and CEO of the company, which he started from his dorm room in 2003. During the company's early years, Jimmy acted as the sole engineer, designer, and support representative for the entire company platform, allowing for it to be a stable business from the outset. In addition to his main responsibilities in running the company and setting overall product strategy, he remains actively involved in the engineering, design, and product teams within the organization. Jimmy holds a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from the University of Maryland.

DESIGN

Design aficionados rarely have any difficulty spotting Scandinavian design, Italian design, and international mid-century classics. But what is modern international design? We think we have found it in the way we curate our collection. The collection itself is a melting pot of pieces by designers from around the world. Furniture Manufacturer's pieces are equally at home with International and European design classics, but we think they express a uniquely International point of view.

CHOICES

Furniture Manufacturer considers our earth's limited resources in the development of its collection. Whenever possible we choose FSC-certified forest products, local resources over, powder coating over high-VOC paints, and recycled/renewable materials for our production. We are proud of our commitment to making the best available choices and will strive to continue to improve. We invite you to ask us about the materials and processes that go into your pieces.
