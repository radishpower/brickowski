Stay With Us. Play With Us in San Francisco.

We are a household of six shaping our daily lives. The use of Brickowski Bricks allows us to figure out what our favorite things are and what sorts of activities help us live best. With Brickowski Bricks, you can spend evenings in our home, buy things we make, and services we provide. You can also follow how we stay active and work towards making our own SF lifestyle. To visit for a few evenings, send an email to someone you know who lives here and/or active@brickowski.com
