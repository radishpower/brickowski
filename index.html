Stay With Us. Play With Us in San Francisco.

We are a household of six shaping our lives in the heart of SF. The use of Brickowski Bricks help us figure out what things and activities we like. With Brickowski Bricks, you can spend evenings in our home, buy things we make, and services we provide. You can also follow how we stay active and how we make our SF lifestyle work. To visit for a few evenings, send an email to someone you know who lives here and/or inquiry@brickowski.com
