Tiled Table

Work in a practical and sophisticated look with Tiled Table, a reconfigurable piece of furniture that shows off your style with tile. Tiled Table gives the owner the ability to show off any four pieces of tile ranging from DIY to the most expensive glass ceramics. A subjective and personal choice.

A versatile piece of heavy duty steel and a strong wooden frame. This sturdy low coffee table you to finish it in your own way and get a distinctive feeling based off the tiles you chose. Comes with clear instructions on how to use standard size tiles. Comes with four basic beige pieces.
